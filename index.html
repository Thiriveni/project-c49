Interesting Facts about Butterfly

  1. Butterfly wings are transparent.
  2. There are almost 20,000 butterfly species.
  3. Butterflies use their feet to taste.
  4. Butterflies only live for a few weeks.


Interesting Facts about Swan

  1. They have their own constellation.
  2. They live for a long time.
  3. They mate for life.
  4. They are huge! Trumpeter swans are the largest native waterfowl and the heaviest flying birds in North America.
